# Resound Studio — Brand Guidelines

---

## Brand Identity

**Resound Studio** (Latin *vox* = voice + *forge* = to create/shape)

An AI Voice Studio that empowers creators to clone, design, and generate ultra-realistic voices — entirely locally, with no cloud dependency.

---

## Tagline

> **AI Voice Studio** — Clone. Design. Generate.

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

- **Professional** but not corporate — confident, modern
- **Technical** but accessible — explain AI concepts simply
- **Empowering** — "your voice studio", "on your device"
- **Privacy-first** — always emphasize local processing
- **Open source** — community-driven, transparent

### Do ✅
- "Clone any voice in 3 seconds"
- "Runs entirely on your hardware"
- "No data leaves your device"
- "Open source and community-driven"

### Don't ❌
- "The best AI ever" (avoid superlatives)
- "Free forever" (avoid promises about pricing)
- Enterprise jargon or buzzword salad
